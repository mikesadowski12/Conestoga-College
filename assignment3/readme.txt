HOW TO INSTALL/USE:

1. One user must compile and run server.c
2. Then they may compile and run client.c
3. Once the server is running, the clients may connect to the server.
4. Then they are to type in the user name they wish to use, and the message. Once enter is pressed, the message
   is sent to the server, and the client disconnects.
5. On the other end, when the 2nd client connects, and sends their message, the entire previous chat log of messages
   will appear on the screen, and they may respond to the other client.
6. Typing CANCEL at anytime as the client will cancel the message being sent. 
7. To close the server, simply kill the terminal or CTRL-C
